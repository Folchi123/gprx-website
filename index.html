Weight Loss & GLP-1 Medication Disclaimer

Greenpoint Pharmacy dispenses FDA-approved GLP-1 receptor agonist medications only upon receipt of a valid prescription from a licensed healthcare provider. Greenpoint Pharmacy does not prescribe, diagnose, or recommend specific medications for weight management.

These statements have not been evaluated by the FDA as they relate to individual patient outcomes. GLP-1 medications must be prescribed by a licensed physician based on individual patient evaluation.

  • GLP-1 medications may not be appropriate for everyone. Contraindications include personal/family history of medullary thyroid carcinoma and Multiple Endocrine Neoplasia syndrome type 2.
  • Common side effects may include nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea. Serious side effects are possible.
  • Individual results vary significantly. Weight loss outcomes are not guaranteed.

Pricing ("as low as $199/month") is subject to change, based on specific medication and dosage, and applies only to eligible patients with a valid prescription.

What Is Compounding?

Medications Made for You — Not the Masses

Pharmaceutical compounding is the art and science of preparing customized medications for individual patients. At Greenpoint Pharmacy, our pharmacist creates formulations not available commercially — in the exact dose, strength, and form prescribed by your doctor.

Whether you need a medication in a different form, without a certain allergen, at a unique strength, or in a flavor a child will actually take — compounding makes it possible.

Pet requires a palatable or alternative dosage form
